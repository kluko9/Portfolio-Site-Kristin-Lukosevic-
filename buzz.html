Buzz

About

The idea behind Buzz was to help couples maximize how they use their budget. When it comes to combining finances it can be scary and difficult. Users can start by setting up their personal budget and inviting their significant other to join the app. Once both have joined there will be a middle section for household expenses. This allows users to combine their bills but still have their own private budget.

The Who, When, Why, and Whats of Buzz

Who

Who will use this app?
Buzz was created for couples and users that share income with other people

When

When will the product be used?
This app will be used every time a user gets paid or needs to update their budget/finances

Why

Why does the user need this product?
Users need this product as it is unique for couples. Buzz's main feature is maintaining a household budget while also maintaining a personal budget with all bank accounts

What

What major goals and tasks will be accomplished throughout this app?

  • Setting up bank accounts and inviting other users to household budget
  • Create budgets for primary bills that the app will recognize as monthly payments
  • Add transactions such as cash payments to ensure budget is up to date
  • View and adjuust both personal budget, and household budget while maintaining users privacy
  • Access all activity, transactions, and budget breakdown

Problem

Keeping track of money and saving is not easy, even users who budget regularly struggle to stay on top of it.

User Interviews

After talking with users there were many similar problems they all faced

  • Budgeting is complicated, especially if you share an account with someone else
  • Users do not feel like they are good at staying on top of their money.
  • Users get lost in their transactions as the money does not always show right away.

Solutions

Based off of user concerns Buzz will have the following features

  • Set up an account and invite other household / couple user
  • Buzz will ensure that their is an individual budget section and a combined budget section
  • Ability to add bank account, transactions will appear in app immediately
  • Option to add an expense such as a cash transaction to ensure money is up to date
  • Expense category so users can add all of their expected monthly bills

Summary

Creating Buzz was a unique experience for me. I have learned many things about financial apps and what they require. This project was the hardest for me to accomplish as I do not feel I have a lot of knowledge in the financial area. Buzz has expanded all of my learning and helped me grow to prepare me for future projects. I thoroughly enjoyed creating all of the icons and making this work for couples.
If I could go back and change Buzz I would create ways for the app to notify you of any false transactions or fraud-like activity. This app is used to help maximize savings and having any sort of hint that something is wrong with finances would be a huge bonus. This was my last project and it has prepared me so much, I cannot wait to create more designs.
