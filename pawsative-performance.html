Pawsative Performance

About

Pawsative performance is a mobile platform focused on dog training. The app combines what your dog needs, your schedule, and your price point to set you up with an ideal trainer. Trainers will meet with you and borrow your pup for 1-hour sessions at all times of the day. The app will give users the ability to chat with trainers, schedule their appointments as well as continue training at home. Its main focus is to work around the owner’s busy schedules.

The Who, When, Why, and Whats of Pawsative Performance

Who

Who will use this app?
This app is intended for users who need training for their dog, such as basic commands and advanced skills/tricks.

When

When will the product be used?
The audience will use this app throughout the day, when they adopt a pup, and when they need basic or advanced training for their pet.

Why

Why does the user need this product?
Users need this product as it will assist their pet throughout their whole growing process. When users get a new pet they require basic commands and tricks. As their pet gets older they can begin to learn more advanced tricks and even have specialty skills. These skills include seeing eye, hunting, or police training. The variety of all tricks and skills in one app makes this app desirable.

What

What major goals and tasks will users complete as they use this app?

  • Having a pet profile to keep up with their pets training status
  • Ability to continue skills at home with quick tips and tricks when pet is not with trainer
  • A way to chat with their trainer for scheduling appointments/ and asking questions about their pet
  • Filters that assist users in finding the best trainer for their based off of tricks, schedule and pricing
  • Have app directly put appointments scheduled into their devices calendar

Problem

The main problem with other dog training apps is they do not always offer all kinds of skill sets. A lot of the time pet owners would also like to continue training at home inbetween sessions.

User Interviews

Throughout the interview process there were a few common problems that needed to be addressed.

  • Users would like the trainers to come to them as they are busy and cannot always bring their pet somewhere.
  • Users would like to choose a trainer based on their specific training requirements.
  • Users would like the option to chat with their trainer throughout the process.
  • Users would like the appointments that are set up to link to their devices' calendar.

Solutions

To fix the common problems in Pawsative Performance the following feature were implemented

  • Filtering options for
    • Trainer ratings
    • Pricing per training session
    • Trainer and dog owners availability
    • Owners house location
  • Users will also be able to request appointment through app, once accepted appointment will be added to their devices calendar
  • App will have dog profile section that will have at home continued learning as well as overall tricks progress
  • Dog owners will be able to chat with their trainers on off session days to ensure their pet is getting the best overall training

Final Product

Here is the final product for Pawsative Performance This training app shows the following

  • Friendly and inviting images
  • Ability to Login or Create account
  • Questionaire section to find the best trainers for your pet
  • Create appointments and add to devices calendar
  • Chat with trainer to discuss pets needs and appointments
  • Check dogs progress on profile page as well as at home training tips

Summary

While working on the design for Pawsative Performance I have acquired many new skills. I now understand guidelines for both IOS and Android devices ensuring that Pawsative Performance will be easy to navigate on each device. The main focus for Pawsative Performance was determined through user research, all users have busy schedules, and having a trainer come to them would be ideal. Implementing the trainer chat feature and calendar setup makes the whole process easier.
If I could recreate this app again or change anything I would add more to the dog’s profile section. As the pet advances in skills perhaps offer awards or print out certificates that can be on their profile.
