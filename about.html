About Me

My Story

Hi, my name is Kristin Lukosevic, I am a 29 year old UX/UI Designer. Before I began designing I was a veterinary technician in an emergency facility and a few specialty hospitals. I specialized in ophthalmology and Cardiology, it was crazy to me how both animals and humans can have a lot of the same illness'. I worked in the field for about 7 years, I loved being the voice for the animals and figuring out their exact needs since they do not have a voice of their own. While saving animals is very fufilling I was still missing my creative desire. After some soul searching UX/UI design was right for me.
When I am not designing I enjoy being outside and hiking. I also enjoy crafting such as wood working and creating new pieces of wall art. Most of the art in my house is something I have created.
